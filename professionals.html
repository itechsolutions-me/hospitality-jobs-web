We're Good At

Easy to use app

Our application is easy to use. In just a few clicks, you can see open positions near you!

Register as a Hospitality Proffesonal

To register is easy. Select 'Register as a Professional'. Provide an email address. Choose a password.

01
  • One time app login
  • Setup your profile
  • Be discoverable on the map

Edit Your Profile

Your profile lets you control what Providers see about you, your location and experience. The more detail you add will generate more interest from a potential new employer.

02
  • Real-time availability list/map
  • Part time availability list/map
  • Detailed profile of you

Looking For Full or Part Time Job

You can select you are available for full or part time jobs or even both. This is an important feature as your profile will appear on the list/map that you mark.

03
  • Real time availability
  • Full time work professionals list/map
  • Part time work professionals list/map

Availability Status

The app has a single action 'toggle' button that controls your availability. You decide your availability in one swipe of the on screen button. If you are unavailable for work providers will not be able to see you, but you can still use the app and search for career change.

04
  • Real time status availability
  • Be (in)visible to providers

Edit Your Specialties

Select your speciality and the sector you are qualified in. The app ranks you based on your location and filters your experience to make it easier for providers to find you.

05
  • Mark the job position that interests you
  • Vacancies list base on your specialities
  • Vacancies list base on your location

Vacancies

The app has a clever feature of only showing you the latest jobs that are closest to you. As long as you add your location and speciality you will see all the vacancies nearest to you. For jobs further away you can select push notification for these career opportunities.

06
  • Contact the employer directly
  • Apply for a job by filling in the form
  • Hassle less job application

Check our Articles

The app gives you access to the latest and most interesting articles related to the catering and hospitality sector.

07
  • Upgrade your career
  • Find inspiring articles
  • Find featured providers
